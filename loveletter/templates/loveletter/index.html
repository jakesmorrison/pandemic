Love Letter Rules

  • Cards 0-4
  • Cards 5-9

Jayne

Choose another player. Give them the Jayne Token. If they win this round, you gain a Token. (1)

Saffron

If you have this card in your hand when another player chooses you as part of a Mal effect, they are out of the round. (1)

Mal

Name a non-Mal card and choose another player. If that player has that card he or she is out of the round. (5)

Book

Look at another player’s hand. (2)

Zoe

You and another player secretly compare hands. The player with the lower value is out of the round. (2)

Kaylee

Until your next turn, ignore all effects from other player’s cards. Or peek at the Hidden Card. (2)

Wash

Wash: Choose any player (including yourself) to discard his or her hand and draw a new card. (2)

Inara

Choose two players (can include yourself). They must trade hands. Look at one of their hands. (1)

Simon

If you have this card and the Wash or Inara card in your hand, you must discard this card. (1)

River

If you discard this card you are out of the round.

Reavers

Name a non-Mal card and choose another player. If they have that card in their hand, gain a Token. River beats the Alliance at the end of the round.
